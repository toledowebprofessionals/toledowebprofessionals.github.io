We are Toledo Web Professionals

We're a grassroots networking organization aimed at creating professional development opportunities, fostering an environment of collaboration, and building a community of technical, design, and business talent in Toledo.

Join & RSVP on Become a Sponsor

We bring learners, professionals, and businesses together.

Toledo is our Mission

Building tech talent in Toledo is what brings us together. We feature discussions on topics ranging from UX and IA to programming and engineering.

We Meet Every Second Wednesday

We facilitate at least one event per month, January through November, on the second Wednesday of the month at our local coworking space, Seed Coworking.

Did We Mention it's Free?

We are run by volunteers, we do not charge any membership fees, and our events are free to attend. In order to provide the best events possible, we depend on our gracious sponsors.
