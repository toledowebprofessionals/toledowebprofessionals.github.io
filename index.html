Call for Sponsors

Sponsors help Toledo Web Professionals to provide the best free Tech Events in the Toledo area. We keep it simple with three levels to help us with our events.

Why Become a Sponsor?

  • Connect with Toledo's Web Community
  • Announce job openings
  • Receive Social Media Promotion
  • We deliver attendees and provide the event management

Join our growing list of premiere sponsors including Owens Corning, Root, Hanson, and MaritzCX.

Which Sponsorship Level is Right for You?

  • Supply Refreshments

    The best networking happens around food which is one of the cornerstones of a great TWP Tech event.

  • Offer a Meeting Space

    The location sponsor provides a place to gather and present including all the right tech to make this happen.

  • Host a Premiere Event

    Premiere sponsors own the show providing both the location and refreshments. Perhaps you have a talk you want to deliver. This is a great way to expose your organization to a vibrant community of developers. You will receive top billing for our promotion plus promote your organization.

Call for Speakers

Do you have something awesome you’d like to share? We’re open to any talk that can be tied to the web, ranging from web design and development, user experience, development tools and best practices, security, infrastructure or whatever you can come up with. We welcome speakers of any experience level.

Have a talk idea? Please send TWP Meetup a note!

About TWP

Toledo Web Professionals is a grassroots organization aimed at bringing professional development opportunities to Toledo, fostering an environment of collaboration and building a community of local web professionals.

We facilitate at least one event per month. Whether it is an event of our own invention or working with other tech groups, we bring outstanding tech talk opportunities to Toledo.

TWP is completely volunteer organized and run. We do not charge any membership fees and our events are free to attend. In order to provide the best events possible we depend on our sponsors.
