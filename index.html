Call for Sponsors

Sponsors help Toledo Web Professionals provide the best Tech Events in the Toledo area. We request three sponsorship levels helping with a location or networking food.

Benefits

  • Connect with Toledo's Web Community
  • Your opportunity to promote your products and services plus announce job openings
  • Facilitation assistance from TWP Meetup
  • Meetup & Social Media Promotion from TWP

Join our growing list of premiere sponsors including Owens Corning, Root, Hanson, and MaritzCX.

Sign Up for our Sponsor Levels

  • Supply Refreshments

    The best networking happens around food which is one of the cornerstones of a great TWP Tech event.

  • Offer a Meeting Space

    The location sponsor provides a place to gather including all the right tech to make this happen.

  • Host a Premiere Event

    Premiere sponsors own the show providing both the location and refreshments. Event sponsorship is a great way to expose your organization to a vibrant community of developers. You will receive top billing on all promotional materials and have the opportunity to promote your organization throughout the event.

Call for Speakers

Do you have something awesome you’d like to share? Share your experience with our community. We’re open to any talk that can be tied to the web, ranging from web design and development, user experience, development tools and best practices, security, infrastructure or whatever you can come up with. We welcome speakers of any experience level and coming from either the Toledo area or traveling in from another location.

Have a talk idea? Please send TWP Meetup a note!

About TWP

Toledo Web Professionals is a grassroots organization aimed at bringing professional development opportunities to Toledo, fostering an environment of collaboration and building a community of local web professionals.

We facilitate at least one event per month and working with other tech groups to bring outstanding development opportunities to Toledo.

TWP is completely volunteer organized and run. We do not charge any membership fees and our events are free to attend. In order to help provide the best events possible we depend on sponsors to help us.
